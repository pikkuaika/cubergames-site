Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Cuber Games respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use CUBER and how that information may be used.

Information We Collect

CUBER does not require you to create an account and does not ask you to provide personal information.

When you use the app, certain technical information may be collected automatically by third-party services, including:

  • Device information
  • Operating system version
  • App performance data
  • Crash reports
  • Anonymous usage statistics
  • Advertising identifiers, where applicable
  • App interactions and diagnostic information

This information may be used to operate the app, improve performance and stability, correct technical issues, measure advertising performance and prevent fraud or misuse.

Advertising

CUBER displays advertisements using Google AdMob.

Google AdMob may collect and process information such as the following, in accordance with applicable laws and Google's policies:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device information
  • Approximate location derived from an IP address
  • App interactions
  • Advertising performance data
  • Diagnostic information

This information may be used to display and measure advertisements, prevent fraud, limit repeated advertisements and improve advertising services.

Where required by applicable law, users will be asked for consent before personalized advertising is displayed. Users may be shown non-personalized or limited advertisements depending on their consent choices and region.

Rewarded Advertisements

CUBER may offer optional rewarded advertisements. For example, a player may choose to watch an advertisement in exchange for an in-game benefit, such as continuing a game after losing.

Watching rewarded advertisements is voluntary. The reward is granted only when the advertising provider confirms that the required advertisement has been completed.

In-App Purchases

CUBER may offer an optional Premium purchase that removes banner advertisements.

All purchases are securely processed through Google Play or another applicable digital storefront. Cuber Games does not collect, process or store payment card details or other payment information.

Purchase information may be checked through the applicable storefront to confirm whether the Premium purchase is active or to restore a previously completed purchase.

Third-Party Services

CUBER may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Play Billing
  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Games, if enabled

These services may collect and process information according to their own privacy policies and terms.

Google Privacy Information

Some features of CUBER rely on Google services. For more information about how Google collects and processes data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

https://policies.google.com/privacy

More information about Google AdMob is available at:

https://support.google.com/admob

Children's Privacy

CUBER is not specifically directed toward children who are below the minimum age required by applicable law.

If you have any questions regarding children's privacy or believe that information has been handled improperly, please contact us at:

support@cubergames.com

Data Retention

Cuber Games does not maintain user accounts or directly store personal information submitted by players.

Information collected by third-party services is retained according to the policies and retention practices of those service providers.

Your Privacy Choices

You can remove banner advertisements by purchasing the optional Premium feature.

Where consent is required by applicable law, users will be asked for consent before personalized advertisements are displayed.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page, and the "Last updated" date will be revised accordingly.
